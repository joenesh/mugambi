What Happens Next

Be Prepared

Trial Day Checklist

Make a great first impression. Here's what to bring:

  • Football boots & shin guards
    Moulded studs or astro shoes — no metal studs.
  • Comfortable training kit
    Shorts, t‑shirt, and long socks. Club kit provided upon joining.
  • Water bottle & towel
    Stay hydrated — we'll have refill stations available.
  • Medical information
    Any relevant allergies, medications, or conditions.
